⚠️ Before you connect a number
Use a new phone number, not your existing WhatsApp number. Connecting a number that already has WhatsApp on it means it can no longer be used in the regular WhatsApp app afterward, and its chat history does not carry over. Also budget a few hours to a few business days for Meta's registration and business verification — don't leave this until the last minute.
